About Us

SIA PaK it provides local and international freight transport — fast, reliable, and efficient.

Our motto: "Keep moving forward!"

With over a decade of hands-on experience, our professional team and modern vehicle fleet enable us to deliver quick, effective solutions for any logistics challenge. We offer a full range of services across the entire supply chain — from transport and warehousing to documentation and customs formalities.

Together with our trusted partners, we handle all types of freight daily for both businesses and private clients across Europe, the United Kingdom, and the Baltic states. Every client matters to us, which is why we provide complete support and end-to-end logistics solutions that help you cut transport costs and stay focused on your core business.
